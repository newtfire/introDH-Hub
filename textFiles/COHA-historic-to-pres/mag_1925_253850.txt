On July 1 , the date the U. S. Immigration Law of 1924 was enacted ( TIME , June 2 , 1924 , et seq . ) , Japan will commemorate Humiliation Day , if the Pacific Civilization Society and kindred organizations have their way . <P> The exercises are to include religious services at the grave of the " Unknown Patriot " who committed hara-kiri ( suicide by evisceration ) near the grounds of the old U. S. Embassy as a protest against " the exclusion of Japanese from the U. S. " Mass meetings and other pacific demonstrations are scheduled and a list of names of representative Japanese , together with their opinions , are to be collected in a book , translated , sent to U. S. Congressmen , Chambers of Commerce , newspapers , etc.
