Microwave ovens differ in length of cooking time and the evenness of cooking just as conventional ranges do . When cooking with a smaller microwave oven , you may have to adjust recipes to account for lower wattage . Check oven directions for recommended cooking times . The microwave recipes that follow were developed for the smaller ovens but we have also included cooking times for larger , 625- to 700-watt microwave oven . If you have a compact oven , check for doneness after the briefest cooking time given in recipe directions . For other microwave ovens , check for doneness midway through the cooking time . Look for the same signs of doneness as in conventional cooking such as tenderness , texture , consistency and temperature . Let that be the final guide rather than an absolute cooking time . Also , bear in mind that the amount of electricity coming into a home depends on whether you live in an urban or rural area .
