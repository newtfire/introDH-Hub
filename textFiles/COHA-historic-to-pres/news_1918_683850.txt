Innovations In soldier ? recreations are recommended by Miss Mary Wood Hinman , official hostess of the state of Illinois , who has returned from a conference on soldier ? welfare at Des Moines , participated in by delegates from ten western states . In ' New York the ban on Sunday dancing has been lifted by church and social organizations to insure clean fun for the soldiers . " A recognized posture for dancing couples and also supervision of the music that is played are found to be a great aid to proper conduct , " said Miss Hinman . Chicago must do more to provide lodging houses , she urges , for " the six thousand boys that pour into the city every Saturday night . " In many of the eastern cities special quarters have been established to accomodate the men . Miss Hinman will submit a report to the war recreation board of Illinois for which she made the trip .
